Detailed Reseller & Client Guide
Learn how credits, voucher generation, the client activation process, and daily auto-like schedules work.

Reseller Workflow (You)

  • 1. Load Credits: Copy your Reseller ID from this dashboard and send it to the admin to top up your USD balance.
  • 2. Generate Key: Fill out the client's game target UID, region, and daily auto-likes delivery time. Duration is fixed at 30 days. Generating a key deducts $2.50 from your credits.
  • 3. Deliver Voucher Link (CRITICAL): When a key is successfully generated, a popup modal displays a Client Activation Link and a VIP Key. You MUST copy and send both this link and the key to your client.
  • 4. Cancel & Refund: If a voucher key is not yet redeemed by the client, you can cancel it at any time in "Activation History" to instantly get a full refund of the key's cost back to your credit balance. Unclaimed keys automatically auto-expire and refund after 7 days.
  • 5. Set Contact Info (REQUIRED): Always fill out the "Contact Settings" section at the bottom. This ensures clients can view your Telegram/WhatsApp/email on their dashboard when they need to renew their plan.

Client Workflow (Your Customer)

  • 1. Open Portal: The client opens the Client Activation Link you provided. They do not need a reseller account; they sign in securely using their own Google account.
  • 2. Redeem Key: The client inputs the VIP Key and clicks "Redeem Voucher". The system verifies the code, locks the target configurations, and activates their VIP status.
  • 3. Bookmark Dashboard (CRITICAL): After redeeming, the client is redirected to their own white-labeled dashboard (e.g., domain.com/YOUR_CODE). They MUST bookmark this link immediately. If they lose it, they cannot access their targets or renew.
  • 4. Automated Daily Delivery: Once the VIP is claimed, our background worker scheduled queue takes over. Likes are automatically sent to the client's game account at their designated send time every day.
  • 5. Upgrades & Renewals: When a client's plan is close to expiring, they can purchase a new voucher key from you, open their dashboard, and redeem it under the "Upgrade Plan" section to instantly extend their VIP duration.
